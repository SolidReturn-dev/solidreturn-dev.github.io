Camera Permission: The camera permission is used for the following purposes: Virtual Meetings: The camera is utilized for virtual meetings within the condominium management service, allowing residents to participate and interact visually.

QR Code Scanning: The camera is used to scan QR codes associated with equipment in the condominium, facilitating the registration and tracking of occurrences.

The recorded files captured by the application serve the following purposes:

Occurrence Illustration: Photos captured by the application aim to visually illustrate the respective occurrences within the condominium. These photos can be used to provide visual context and support for incident reports or documentation.

Data Encryption: We utilize industry-standard encryption protocols to protect the confidentiality of the stored data. This encryption safeguards the data from unauthorized access or interception during transmission and while stored in our systems.

Security Measures: We employ technical and organizational measures to maintain the security of the information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Regular security assessments and updates are performed to ensure the ongoing effectiveness of our security measures.

User Responsibility: Users are encouraged to choose strong and unique passwords and to keep them confidential. It is important to protect personal login credentials to prevent unauthorized access to personal data within the application.
